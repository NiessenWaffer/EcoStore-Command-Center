# Unified User Journey: The Circular Economy Platform

This document maps the end-to-end user experience across the 18 implemented plans, representing a professional, cryptographically secured circular economy ecosystem.

## 1. Discovery & Radical Transparency (Plans 1, 3, 8)
- **Journey:** User arrives at the mobile-first Home Page.
- **Experience:** They explore the Shop, where every product displays an **Impact Index (0-100)** and a **Regenerative Tier**.
- **Truth:** Data is driven by real-time material composition math (Hemp, Organic Cotton, Recycled Poly).

## 2. Interactive Decision Support (Plans 4, 15)
- **Journey:** User views a Product Detail Page (PDP).
- **Experience:** They use the **AI Stylist** for personalized recommendations and the **Fit Wizard** to reduce return rates.
- **Verification:** They view the **Unforgeable Product Passport**, seeing the exact material source and factory origin.

## 3. Truth-Driven Checkout (Plans 1, 5, 12, 18)
- **Journey:** User adds items to the cart and enters a shipping address.
- **Experience:** The system calculates a real **Carbon Gate Fee** based on the Haversine distance from the nearest **Local Hub** (NYC, London, Tokyo, Berlin).
- **Impact:** Mandatory regeneration fees ($0.15/kg CO2) are applied to international transit.

## 4. The Ambassador Portfolio (Plans 2, 11, 16, 18)
- **Journey:** User creates an account and views their Dashboard.
- **Experience:** They see a live **Impact Pulse** chart (real-time SQL aggregation of their water/carbon savings) and unlock **Milestone Badges** (Water Guardian, Carbon Neutral).
- **Growth:** They manage their **Ambassador Referral Code** to grow the network impact.

## 5. Circular Ownership & Resale (Plans 3, 9, 18)
- **Journey:** User decides to trade-in a past purchase.
- **Experience:** They use the **Resale Portal** to initiate a trade-in for store credit.
- **Trust:** They can transfer ownership of their Product Passport to another user via a **P2P Claim Token**, ensuring a cryptographically verified chain of custody.

## 6. Democratic Governance (Plans 7, 11, 18)
- **Journey:** User participates in brand direction.
- **Experience:** They use **Quadratic Voting** ($influence = \sqrt{cost}$) to vote on impact fund allocations or product corrections.
- **Integrity:** Quorum progress is tracked live based on the total resultant influence of the community.

## 7. The Command Center (Plans 10, 13, 17, 18)
- **Journey:** Admin logs into the Ecosystem Pulse dashboard.
- **Experience:** High-density KPIs monitor global water savings, system health (tampered vs. verified passports), and active governance.
- **Signature:** Every action is traceable, and the platform bears the "Created by Ronie R. Pactol" attribution.
